About Me

The person behind ClearWebSolutions — and why I built this.

Founder

ClearWebSolutions

Built for the businesses that deserve better

I started this company with one simple goal in mind — to give smaller, growing businesses a real fighting chance in the digital world.

I noticed that so many hardworking companies were stuck with outdated websites, not because they didn't care, but because the big corporate web design agencies charge prices that just aren't realistic for a small business trying to grow. That never sat right with me.

Every business, no matter the size, deserves a website that actually represents them well and helps them succeed — so I decided to do something about it.

This company was built to be the affordable, honest alternative. We reach out to small businesses, redesign their websites at a price that makes sense, and deliver something they can actually be proud of.

Passion-Driven

Web design is more than making things look pretty — it's about giving a business the online presence it needs to grow, compete, and be taken seriously.

Honest & Affordable

We don't charge the outrageous prices of big agencies. Our goal is to deliver real value at a price that works for businesses that are still growing.

Built on Trust
